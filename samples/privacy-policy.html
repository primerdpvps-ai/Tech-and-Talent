1. Data Collection

We collect personal information necessary for providing our freelance platform services, including:

  • Identity Data: Name, address, date of birth, national ID/tax information.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, physical address.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, payment information (for payroll/billing).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, usage data, and location data.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Verify user identity and eligibility for services.
  • Process transactions and manage payments/payroll.
  • Improve and personalize the platform experience.
  • Communicate important updates and security notices.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With **payment processors** to facilitate billing and payouts.
  • With **contractors/clients** to facilitate service contracts (e.g., sharing contact info).
  • When required by **law** or legal processes in Pakistan.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including:

  • Encryption for all data transmission and storage.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for secure logins.
  • Admin-only access to sensitive payroll records; access logging for security events.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention).
  • Withdraw consent by requesting account deactivation.
  • Submit questions or complaints via the Contact section.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain secure sessions and enhance dashboard UX. You may disable cookies in your browser; some features may not function.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data while your account is active and for as long as necessary to meet legal obligations. Data is securely deleted when no longer required.

8. Third-Party Links

External links may be present. We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. We will notify you by email or dashboard notice. Continued use indicates acceptance of changes.
